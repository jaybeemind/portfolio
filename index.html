About Me

I'm a senior full-stack developer from the Philippines with over 12 years of experience building real-world business applications across backend, frontend, databases, and automation workflows.

My strongest work is in practical business systems: internal tools, process automation, API-driven platforms, dashboards, integrations, and database-backed applications that reflect how teams actually operate.
